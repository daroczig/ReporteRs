ReporteRs

An R package to generate Word, PowerPoint or HTML reports.

PowerPoint documents

Create your own Powerpoint presentations from R. Use your corporate template. Design your layout in PowerPoint. Add or replace slides in existing Powerpoint documents.

More

Word documents

Create your own Word documents from R. Use your corporate template. Insert table of contents, editable vector graphics, paragraphs etc. Replace content in existing Word documents.

More

HTML documents

Publish your reports from R in a WEB format. Organize your content within HTML pages. Use a predefined bootstrap layout.
